About

Lars E. Schonander is a software engineer at Lincoln Network, helping non-profits across D.C. and beyond with their various tech problems, from web development to data analysis.

What I do on a day to day basis can be described as helping other think-tanks with their technical problems. This ranges from building websites with HTML/CSS/JavaScript and other tools building out backend infrastructure with languages like Python, or doing data analysis with languages like Python or R. In practice I do quite a bit more, but that's the gist of it.

I run a newsletter called The Aggregate, focused on the analysis of unusual datasets. There is a link to subscribe on the home page. You should check it out.
